Self-Gift is Here!

Take a mic seat, select your own avatar, and send gifts to yourself in the room.

What is Self-Gift?

When you are sitting on a mic seat, your own avatar will appear in the gift recipient area. Select your avatar and send a gift just like you would send gifts to others.

On Mic

Take a mic seat in the room

›

Select Yourself

Choose your own avatar

›

Send Gift

Send a gift to yourself

Important Requirement

You need to be on a mic seat first. Your avatar will appear in the gift recipient list after you take a mic seat.

How to Send a Gift to Yourself

1

Take a mic seat

Sit on an empty mic seat.

★ Key step
2

Select your avatar

Choose the Me avatar in the gift panel.

3

Send a gift

Pick a gift and tap Send.

Please Note

Self-gifting uses your gift balance as normal. Please make sure you have enough balance before sending. Gift effects will be displayed in the voice room after sending.

Trade Function Notice

With Self-Gift now available, the in-app Trade function has been discontinued. Agents and coin sellers can contact their admin to apply for Payroll permission.
